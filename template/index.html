People

We have a truly professional, internationally qualified head instructor and our love for teaching from beginners to the most experienced English riders. Our head instructor is a full-time, experienced professional with FEI (Federation Equestre Internationale) recognized qualifications, with international certifications and awards. We love horses and riding, and we want to make sure you have an experience which not only teaches you how to ride but also shares your joy and excitement at horsemanship.
